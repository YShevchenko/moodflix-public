MoodFlix - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Publisher: Heldig Lab

Contact: heldig.lab@pm.me

Overview

MoodFlix is a movie and series discovery app. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

MoodFlix stores the following data on secure cloud servers (Firebase) to enable its recommendation and personalization features:

  • User preferences — selected streaming services, genres, content era preferences
  • Swipe history — titles you swiped right (liked) or left (skipped)
  • Watchlist — titles you saved to watch later
  • Watch history — titles you marked as watched
  • Search history — mood-based search queries used to improve recommendations

This data is stored on your account and used solely to power MoodFlix recommendations. We do not sell or share this data with third parties for advertising purposes.

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google) — cloud database and backend functions. Google's privacy policy applies: firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • TMDB (The Movie Database) — movie and series metadata. TMDB's terms apply: themoviedb.org/privacy-policy
  • Google AdMob — in-app advertising (free tier users only). AdMob may collect device identifiers for ad personalization. See: policies.google.com/privacy

Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained as long as you use MoodFlix. You can delete all your account data at any time from Settings → Delete Account. This permanently removes your profile, preferences, swipe history, and watchlist from our servers.

Children's Privacy

MoodFlix is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated in the app.
